The Perfect Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is my favorite dessert. For more information, check out this similar recipe .

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Packs of Cream Cheese

Directions

  1. Heat Oven 325 Degrees Fahrenheit
  2. bet Cream Chess, Sugar, Vanilla with mixer until blended. Add eggs and beat until blended
  3. Pour into crust.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes or until center is almost set. cool. Refrigerate for 3 hours
